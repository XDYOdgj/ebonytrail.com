Transform Your Floor Cleaning with the Ewbank EP170

The Ewbank EP170 is a powerful 3-in-1 floor cleaner that simplifies your cleaning routine. Whether you're looking for daily maintenance, deep scrubbing, or final polishing, this machine delivers exceptional results with ease. Designed for versatility, it handles a wide range of hard floor surfaces like laminate, wood, vinyl, marble, and granite, making it the ultimate choice for any home.

Efficient and Powerful Cleaning

Equipped with contra-rotating discs that spin at an impressive 2,200 rpm, the EP170 ensures deep cleaning without damaging your floors. The high-speed performance works in harmony with its wide 9.5-inch cleaning path, allowing you to cover large areas quickly and efficiently. This makes it perfect for high-traffic zones, bathrooms, and living rooms where a spotless finish is essential.

Customizable Attachments for Every Surface

The EP170 comes with five interchangeable brushes and pads, giving you the flexibility to adapt to different floor types and tasks. From scrubbing stubborn stains to polishing for a mirror-like shine, these attachments transform the machine into a multi-functional tool that meets all your cleaning needs.

Comfort and Control in Every Detail

Its ergonomic telescopic handle features a comfortable butterfly grip, making it easy to handle during long cleaning sessions. The lightweight design, under 8 lbs, allows for effortless maneuverability, reducing strain and making the job more enjoyable. Plus, the built-in cable storage keeps your workspace organized and clutter-free.

Built to Last with Durable Construction

The EP170 is built with high-impact durable materials, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability. Whether you're a busy homeowner or a professional cleaner, this machine is designed to handle frequent use without compromising on quality. Its robust build and consistent results make it a worthy investment for anyone who values efficiency and effectiveness in floor care.
